What Are GI Tags?

Geographical Indications —
The Mark of True Origin

Geographical Indications are legal marks that show where something truly comes from — proving its origin, authenticity, and ownership, tied to a specific place, its craft, and its people.

Protects Authenticity

Ensures every piece carries its true origin mark

Builds Value

Raises global market worth of handcrafted goods

Prevents Imitation

Legal shield against counterfeit products globally

Honours Origin

Celebrates land, people, and craft traditions
